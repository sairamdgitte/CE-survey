ABOUT THE SURVEY

The City of Melbourne together with RMIT University has plans to develop a small area around RMIT's CBD North campus to foster social impact and innovation. As part of this process, RMIT aims to include Sustainability and Circular Economy principles within this development. Research is being conducted among key groups who work, study, and operate businesses in this area.
